Anodized instrument panel

Brushed-metal panels tinted in soft anodized pastels instead of gunmetal grey. Tight machined corners, one accent spent per screen, status carried by small LED dots. Same tokens, same components, applied consistently across the sidebar desktop layout and the mobile drawer layout.
